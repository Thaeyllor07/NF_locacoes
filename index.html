About Us

Who We Are

Founded by CEO Marclesio Teixeira, MTX Construction was created with a purpose far beyond construction — carrying forward a family legacy built on hard work, honesty, and dedication. The name “MTX” comes from the initials of Marclesio and the “TX” from Teixeira, honoring his father’s legacy and values. With over 10+ years of experience serving the Boston region, MTX Construction has become known for delivering high-quality craftsmanship, fair pricing, and projects completed on schedule. From renovations to custom construction projects, our mission is to provide reliable service and create spaces that exceed expectations.

Today, MTX continues to grow as a solid and trusted company, earning the satisfaction and confidence of clients and friends through professionalism, commitment, and attention to every detail.
